About the event

A3 Scotland 2027

A3 Scotland 2027 will bring together senior leaders from across the three As — Agritech, Aquaculture and Animal Health — to explore how Scotland can help shape the future of food security, climate resilience, export growth and international innovation.

Hosted at the James Hutton Institute in Dundee, the event will connect industry, government, investors, researchers and international partners around practical opportunities across sustainable food systems, digital agriculture, controlled environment agriculture, breeding, aquaculture, animal health innovation and regional economic growth.
